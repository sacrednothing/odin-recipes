Cappucino Vegan Cups

Coffee flavored chocolate and cream cups. Completely vegan!

Decadent dark chocolate cups filled with a layer of coffee flavored chocolate, topped with a layer of creamy cashew "milk".

Ingredients

  • 250 g dark (70%) chocolate, divided
  • 1 cup cashews (soaked overnight)
  • 6 Tbsp coconut cream
  • 3-4 Tbsp instant coffee (adjust to your liking)
  • A few drops of vanilla essence (optional)
  • 1 Tbsp Dutch cocoa powder or chocolate shavings (optional), for decoration
